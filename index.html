Understanding TRON SRs

What is an SR?

Super Representatives (SRs) are the backbone of the TRON network. They validate transactions and generate new blocks. There are only 27 active SRs at any time, chosen by the community.

Why Vote?

When you vote for an SR, you help secure the network. In exchange, SRs share the block rewards they earn with their voters. It is a way to grow your TRX holdings passively.

80% Reward Ratio

DigitalCurrencyDaily is committed to the community. We distribute 80% of generated rewards back to our voters, keeping only 20% for server maintenance and project growth.
