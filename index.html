What is a TRON Super Representative?

The TRON network uses a **Delegated Proof of Stake (DPoS)** consensus mechanism. Unlike mining, TRX holders vote for 27 **Super Representatives (SRs)** who are responsible for validating transactions and producing blocks. By voting for DigitalCurrencyDaily, you participate in network security and earn a share of the block rewards.

How to Vote & Earn

  1. Install TronLink: Download the extension or mobile app and fund it with TRX.
  2. Stake (Freeze) TRX: Click "Stake" in TronLink. Choose **Stake 2.0** to freeze TRX for either **Energy** or **Bandwidth**. This generates **TRON Power**.
  3. Vote: Go to the "Vote" section. Search for the address:
    TWKFjPakHP54EAXTMifbKA77RT3g8cyZoQ
  4. Earn Rewards: Once you cast your votes, you will automatically start earning TRX rewards based on the SR's distribution ratio.
